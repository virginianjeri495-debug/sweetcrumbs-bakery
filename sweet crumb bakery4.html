About Us

SweetCrumbs was Founded in 2010 by Grace &Samuel Kariuki with a single oven,a family recipe book and a dream to bring authentic,artisan baking to the hearts of Nairobi.

over the years ,we have grown into a beloved neighbourhood bakery trusted by thousands of families, and event plan ners across the city.Our bakery rise before dawn every single day to ensure what land on your table is alwways fresh,always flavourful, and always made with care.

We believe baking is an art,not a factory process.That is why we never use artificial presevertives, and we source our flour , eggs , and dairy from certified local farm 100KM of Nairobi
